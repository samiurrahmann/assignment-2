Fitness

Fitness is the state of being physically healthy and strong, achieved through regular exercise, a balanced diet, and healthy lifestyle choices. It involves cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, flexibility, and body composition. Engaging in physical activities enhances overall fitness, mental well-being, energy levels, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases, contributing to a better quality of life.
